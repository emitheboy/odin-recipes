Simple Mexican Elote Snack

Prepare a simple treat in 10min

Authentic mexican elote recipe to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredient

  • Elote
  • Tajin
  • cheese powder
  • mayo
  • lime
  • Salt

Steps to Preparing

  1. Place water in pot and heat until boil
  2. place corn into hot boiling water,let corn cook for 10min
  3. Drain Elote
  4. Spread mayo on elote first
  5. add cheese powder
  6. add tajin spice
  7. Finish off with fresh squeeze lime

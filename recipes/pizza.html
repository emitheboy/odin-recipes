Pepperoni Pizza

Prepare your very own hot and fresh pizza pie at home

Simple and robust pizza recipe that will have you asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese,grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • flour for rliling and shaping dough

Steps to cooking

  1. Preheat the oven to 500F
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Rlil out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve

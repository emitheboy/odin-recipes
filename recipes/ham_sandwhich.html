Simple Ham Sandwhich

Prepare a simple tasty ham sandwhich

Percect ham sandwhich for any occasion

Ingredient

  • Two slices of bread
  • Ham slices
  • Sliced pickels
  • 2 Lettuce Pieces
  • Mayo
  • Mustard

Steps to preparing

  1. spread mayo on each slice of bread evenly
  2. Place thin slices of ham on bread
  3. place sliced pickels and lettuce pieces
  4. place second piece of bread ontop
